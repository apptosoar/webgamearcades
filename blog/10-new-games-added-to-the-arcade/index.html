10 New Games Added to the Arcade

We just shipped ten new browser games — five action titles and five puzzles. They cover a wider range of mechanics than our previous batches: typing speed, visual rhythm, gravity, color matching, maze navigation, arithmetic, bubble chains, sliding ice puzzles, memory cards, and a balance scale. All ten are free to play right now, no install or account required.

Action Games

Typing Rush

Action

A word appears on screen with a shrinking timer bar. Type it correctly before the bar runs out. Words get longer and time shrinks with each level. At level five you have two seconds for a six-letter word — it gets tight fast.

Play Typing Rush →

Color Match

Action

A target color glows at the top of the screen. Balls of various colors fall toward your paddle. Catch balls that match the target — catching the wrong color or missing a matching one costs a life. The target color switches every five correct catches.

Play Color Match →

Snake Maze

Action

Classic snake, but the grid has real internal walls. Two full-width barriers and two vertical barriers divide the board into five zones. You can only cross between zones through single-cell gaps, so route planning matters far more than open-grid snake.

Play Snake Maze →

Gravity Flip

Action

Your triangle moves right automatically. Tap or press Space to flip gravity — the player falls upward instead of down. Purple obstacle pairs scroll in from the right. The longer you survive, the faster they come.

Play Gravity Flip →

Rhythm Tap

Action

Four colored lanes, notes falling from the top. Press D, F, J, K on a keyboard or tap the lanes on a phone to hit notes as they reach the target line. Perfect hits score more than near-hits, and a miss breaks your combo multiplier.

Play Rhythm Tap →

Puzzle Games

Number Crunch

Puzzle

An equation appears with four answer choices. Pick the right one before the timer runs out. Early levels cover addition and subtraction; later levels add multiplication and division. The faster you answer, the higher your bonus points.

Play Number Crunch →

Bubble Pop

Puzzle

Click groups of two or more same-color bubbles to pop them. Larger groups score more. New rows push in from the top periodically — let any bubble cross the danger line and the game ends. Chains and combos are the key to high scores.

Play Bubble Pop →

Ice Slide

Puzzle

A penguin on frictionless ice slides until it hits a wall. Fifteen handcrafted levels. Guide the penguin to the star using walls as stopping points to change direction. Later levels require four or more slides and precise sequencing to reach the goal.

Play Ice Slide →

Card Flip

Puzzle

Sixteen face-down cards hide eight emoji pairs. Flip two at a time — a match stays face up, a mismatch flips back. Find all eight pairs before the 60-second timer runs out. Score is based on time remaining minus a penalty for extra flips.

Play Card Flip →

Tower Balance

Puzzle

A seesaw beam sits on a central pivot. Each round shows a block labeled with its weight — 1, 2, or 3. Press Left or Right to place it on the matching side. The beam tilts with every weight difference. Let it tilt past 60 degrees and the game ends.

Play Tower Balance →
