About Webgame Arcades

Webgame Arcades is a free collection of browser-based mini games that run instantly — no download, no install, no account required. Just open a game and play.

The site is designed to be fast, clean, and mobile-friendly. Every game works on desktop and touchscreen devices, so you can play wherever you are.

What we offer

  • Action games — arena shooters, runners, and dodge challenges
  • Puzzle games — Sokoban, maze navigation, tile matching, and more
  • Casual games — clickers, washing simulators, painting races
  • New games added regularly

Our principles

All games are completely free — no payments, subscriptions, or hidden fees. The site is funded by advertising. We keep ads clearly separated from game controls so they never interfere with gameplay.

We take privacy seriously. We do not collect personal data beyond what is needed to operate the site and serve ads. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
