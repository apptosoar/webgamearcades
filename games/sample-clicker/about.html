Sample Clicker

A simple, satisfying clicker. Hit the button to increase your score — every 10 clicks triggers a Bonus burst with a shower of colourful particles. There's no end goal, just pure clicking flow.

How to Play

  • Click / Tap the button to score a point
  • Every 10 clicks triggers a colourful particle burst
  • Press Restart to reset the score and start over
  • Fully touch-friendly on mobile

About This Game

Sample Clicker is the purest form of a clicker game: one button, one score, infinite clicks. Every tenth click triggers a Bonus burst — a shower of colourful particles that explodes from your tap point. There's no end state, no goal to reach, just the satisfying loop of click, score, repeat.

A great way to take a quick mental break. Click as fast as you can and watch the score climb.

Why We Built It

This game started as a template demonstrating how to load an external HTML game file into the Webgame Arcades framework. It evolved into a proper standalone clicker because the particle burst effect was too satisfying to leave as a placeholder. The multilingual support (15 languages auto-detected from browser settings) was added to show how locale-aware browser games can work without a backend.

How It Works

Each click increments a counter and checks whether it's a multiple of 10 to trigger the Bonus label. The particle burst creates 18 individual DOM elements per click, each assigned a random angle, distance, color, shape, and spin via CSS custom properties. They animate outward using a CSS keyframe and remove themselves from the DOM when the animation ends. Locale detection reads navigator.languages and maps the first matching prefix to one of 15 supported language codes.

Tips & Strategy

  • Click in a steady rhythm rather than bursting — you'll hit higher counts with less fatigue.
  • Try landing clicks at different positions on the button to see the particle burst origin shift.
  • Use Restart to reset and challenge your previous session's score.
  • On mobile, a light single-finger tap works best — speed is the only variable.

Ideas for Improvement

  • Click-per-second display — a live readout showing your current tapping rate over the last second.
  • Milestone rewards — visual changes to the button or background at 100, 500, and 1000 clicks.
  • Auto-clicker upgrade — spend accumulated score to unlock an idle click rate that ticks even when you're not pressing.
  • High score storage — localStorage persistence that shows your all-time best score across sessions.
