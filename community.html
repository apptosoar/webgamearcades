Community

Connect with other Webgame Arcades players — share high scores, suggest new games, and let us know what you'd like to see next.

Share your scores

Beat your personal best? Found a strategy that works? We want to hear about it. Send your high scores, tips, or game feedback directly to us and we'll feature the best ones here.

Suggest a game

Have an idea for a game you'd like to see on the site? We take suggestions seriously — several games in the current library started as player requests. Tell us the mechanic, the genre, or even just the feeling you're looking for.

Report a bug

Found something broken? A game that won't load, a control that doesn't respond, or a score that didn't save? Let us know which game, which browser, and what happened. Bug reports go directly to the developer.

Get in touch

All community messages go through the Contact page. We read every message and reply to as many as we can. A dedicated community space is in the works — until then, email is the best way to reach us.
